# The Game Beyond Podcast

The Game Beyond podcast is an educational podcast about game design, serious games, player psychology, systems thinking, and the craft of designing meaningful interactive experiences.

Podcast:
https://www.thegamebeyond.com/podcast

## Podcast Purpose

The podcast helps listeners understand game design concepts in an accessible, conversational format. It supports The Game Beyond's broader mission of teaching students how to create games that entertain, educate, inspire, and solve real-world problems.

## Season 2

Season 2 is a 10-part podcast season that guides listeners through The Game Beyond master game design framework for making serious games.

It is designed for:

- Beginner game designers
- University students
- Indie developers
- Serious game designers
- Teachers and educators
- Game design hobbyists

## Core Topics

Podcast topics include:

- Serious game design
- Cognitive surplus
- Player psychology
- Flow state
- Game progression
- Goals
- Rewards
- Feedback
- Motivation
- Systems thinking
- Design iteration
- Games as tools for learning and change

## Recommended Episodes

### Episode 1

Introduces the idea that players can become builders and that game design can redirect human attention and creativity toward solving meaningful problems.

### Episode 3

Explores the psychology of the hook and flow state. Useful for users asking why games are engaging or how designers balance challenge and skill.

### Episode 4

Explores progression systems and why simple objectives can work when they create clear player direction and momentum.

### Episode 5

Explores layered goals and how short-term, mid-term, and long-term goals keep players engaged.

### Episode 6

Explores rewards, motivation, and how rewards shape player behavior.
